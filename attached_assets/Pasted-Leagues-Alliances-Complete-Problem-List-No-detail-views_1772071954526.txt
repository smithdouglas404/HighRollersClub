Leagues & Alliances — Complete Problem List:

No detail views at all — You can see a list of alliances and league seasons, but clicking on any of them does nothing. No detail page exists.
No single-item API routes — Backend only has GET /api/alliances (list all) and GET /api/leagues (list all). No GET /api/alliances/:id or GET /api/leagues/:id for details.
Alliance cards are dead — The ChevronRight icon on each club inside an alliance is purely decorative. No click handler, no navigation.
No "Join Alliance" flow — You can only create an alliance. There's no way for another club to join an existing one.
Alliance creation is blind — It auto-adds clubs[0] as the founding member with no way to invite other clubs.
No club info shown in alliances — Alliance cards show club names and member counts, but there's no way to click through to see the club's profile or details.
League standings are empty — Standings are stored as JSON, but there's no mechanism to populate them from actual gameplay. The updateLeagueStandings storage method exists but is never called from any route or game logic.
No alliance info on Club Dashboard — Your club's alliance membership isn't shown anywhere on the club page. You have to go to /leagues to discover it.
No way to manage alliances — No edit, delete, add/remove clubs, or any management UI after creation.
No league lifecycle — Leagues can be created with start/end dates, but nothing happens when they start or end. No auto-status changes, no prize distribution.
