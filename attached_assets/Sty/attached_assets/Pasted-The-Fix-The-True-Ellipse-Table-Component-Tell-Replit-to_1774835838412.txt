How to get the "Vibe" back in Replit:

The Aspect Ratio: Notice I used aspect-[2/1]. That is what kills the circle. It forces the width to be double the height.

The Borders: I used rounded-[100%]. On a non-square container, this creates a perfect ellipse.

The Perspective: Tell Replit: "Position the top players at top: -10% and the bottom players at bottom: -10% of the table container so they look like they are sitting 'behind' and 'in front' of the felt."
