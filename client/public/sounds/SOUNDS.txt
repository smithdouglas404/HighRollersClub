CUSTOM SOUND EFFECTS
====================

Drop your own sound files into this folder to replace the default synthesized sounds.
The game will automatically use your files instead of the built-in generated sounds.

SUPPORTED FORMATS (in order of preference):
  - MP3  (.mp3)  — Best compatibility, small file size. RECOMMENDED.
  - WAV  (.wav)  — Lossless, larger files but zero decoding delay.
  - OGG  (.ogg)  — Good quality/size ratio, not supported on Safari.
  - M4A  (.m4a)  — Good quality, Apple-friendly.
  - WEBM (.webm) — Modern format, good compression.

FILE NAMES:
  Name your files exactly as listed below (without extension shown):

  card-deal        — Card being dealt / sliding across felt
  card-flip        — Card flipping over (community card reveal)
  chip-clink       — Single chip clink / stack sound
  chip-slide       — Chips being pushed across the table
  fold             — Player folds
  check            — Player checks (table knock)
  call             — Player calls
  raise            — Player raises
  phase-reveal     — New community cards phase (flop/turn/river whoosh)
  showdown         — Showdown fanfare
  win              — Winner celebration
  timer-tick       — Turn timer tick (keep it short, < 0.1s)
  turn-notify      — "Your turn" notification chime
  countdown        — Hand starting countdown beep (3, 2, 1...)

EXAMPLES:
  card-deal.mp3
  chip-clink.wav
  showdown.mp3
  countdown.mp3

TIPS:
  - Keep SFX files short (under 2 seconds for most, under 5s for win/showdown)
  - Normalize volume levels across all files for consistent experience
  - The timer-tick sound plays rapidly — keep it very short (50-100ms)
  - The countdown sound plays once per second during the pre-hand countdown
